The Main Characters in Beowulf:

  • Beowulf

    Beowulf is a Geatish hero who fights the monster Grendel, Grendel's mother and the dragon.

  • King Hrothgar

    The king of the Danes, Hrothgar is very successful, at least until Grendel attacks his realm. Hrothgar is a father figure to and model for Beowulf.

  • Grendel

    Grendel is a monster who Beowulf has to fight. Grendel is the first antagonist of the story.

  • Grendel's Mother

    Grendel's mother is the second antagonist of the story, who wants to avenge her son's death.

  • The Dragon

    The dragon is the final antagonist of the story, who wants to get revenge on a escaped servant for stealing from the dragon's hoard.

  • Hygelac

    Beowulf's uncle and king of the Geats.

  • Wiglaf

    A young retainer of Beowulf who helps him in the fight against the dragon, while all of the other warriors run away.
